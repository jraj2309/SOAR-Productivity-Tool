To use first scroll down to the pomodoro section. There you will see a pomodoro timer, a sound player, a to-do list, and a background changer.
These are there to change your enviorment to cater to your feelings, therefore maximizing productivity. Below are some tips when using SOAR


1. In your 5 minute breaks don't use the computer.

2. Use the music to calm yourself.

3. In the case you do get distracted, take a long break away from the screen.

4. Self reflect on the day to see if you were efficent.

5. Make goals everyday to improve upon something. This will assist with your productivity.

6. After finished with your work, reward yourself by taking a long break.

7. Make sure you are truly interested in your work. Align your personal goals and interests, and incorporate them into your lifestyle

8. Truly understand the goal of your work, so you can build passion

9. Make sure you are commited to studying to build momentum

10. Start with the easiest task, then build up into harder ones, and finally finish with the easiest tasks.

11. Use HEADPHONES
